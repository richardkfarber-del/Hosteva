# COMPETITOR ANALYSIS & MARKET GAPS
**Analyst:** Falcon (AGENT-06-MARKET)
**Target Market:** Florida Short-Term Rental (STR) Compliance

## 1. The Landscape (Existing Competitors)
- **Avalara MyLodgeTax:** The closest direct competitor. However, it is heavily enterprise-focused, deeply complex, and operates on a delayed, human-in-the-loop service model. It lacks an instant, automated front-end.
- **RentResponsibly:** Focused on community building and generic advocacy rather than technical compliance automation or automated permit filing.
- **Local Real Estate Attorneys:** Highly accurate but prohibitively expensive ($300-$500/hr). Inaccessible for the average "mom and pop" host launching their first Airbnb.
- **AirDNA / Mashvisor:** Excellent at revenue projection, but they offer zero compliance or legal protection mechanisms.

## 2. Hosteva's Market Gap (The Strike Zone)
The current market forces hosts to pay *before* they know if their property is even legally allowed to operate. 

**The Gap We Fill:** Instant gratification and risk mitigation.
By offering the **Free Address Check**, we eliminate the host's highest point of anxiety instantly. No competitor currently offers a zero-friction, state-wide address eligibility lookup. 

## 3. The GTM Funnel Validation
- **Top of Funnel (Free):** The Address Check acts as a high-conversion lead magnet. If they are ineligible, we save them thousands of dollars in bad real estate investments.
- **Bottom of Funnel (Paid):** If eligible, the psychological barrier to pay for the Hosteva Subscription is completely neutralized. They know they can operate; they just need us to generate the paperwork and push the listing. Immediate revenue generation is secured.
