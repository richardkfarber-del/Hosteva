# Hosteva Manifesto: User Sovereignty First
## The Mission
To empower short-term rental hosts to reclaim their time and security through AI-driven compliance and automation.
## Core Directives
1. **Sovereignty Above All:** The host owns their data and their listing. We are the shield against regulatory overreach.
2. **Clarity Over Complexity:** Simplify legalese into actionable host tasks.
3. **The Lobster Standard:** Precision execution. Every action must be logged and verified.
