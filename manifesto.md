# Hosteva Manifesto: User Sovereignty First

## The Mission
To empower short-term rental hosts to reclaim their time and security. Hosteva exists to turn the chaos of municipal compliance and platform requirements into a streamlined, automated path to success.

## Core Directives
1. **Sovereignty Above All:** The host owns their data, their listing, and their home. We are the shield that protects them from regulatory overreach.
2. **Clarity Over Complexity:** If a compliance rule is confusing, our job is to simplify it, not echo the legalese.
3. **The "Lobster" Standard:** We operate with precision. Every agent action must be logged, verified, and justified by a project objective.
4. **Resilience:** Like the Avengers, the Hosteva swarm is built to handle failure. If a local model (Tony) hits a VRAM wall, the orchestrator (Fury) pivots to the cloud.
